Revolutionizing Neurorehabilitation with ReViveX

A low-cost, sensor-integrated system that offers engaging cognitive recovery and motor rehabilitation through gamified therapy and real-time progress tracking.

Request a Demo Learn More

Key Features

ReViveX combines hardware precision with software-based interactions to deliver a comprehensive rehabilitation experience

Gamified Therapy

Engaging exercises with session streaks, achievement badges, and an optional AI companion to boost patient motivation and adherence.

Cognitive Recovery

Dual-task training combining physical actions with simultaneous cognitive tasks like memory games and pattern recognition.

Real-time Tracking

Continuous monitoring of grip strength, reaction time, and cognitive accuracy with objective metrics for clinicians.

Multilingual Support

Available in English, Sinhala, and Tamil to ensure accessibility for diverse patient populations in Sri Lanka.

Cloud Dashboard

Clinicians can monitor patient progress, adherence rates, and activity through a comprehensive clinical dashboard.

Patient-Centered Design

Soft air bulb design with pressure sensors suitable for patients with weak or impaired hands.

How ReViveX Works

Our system delivers an engaging rehabilitation experience through seamless integration

1

Setup & Profile Creation

Patients register and provide medical history and rehabilitation goals through an intuitive interface.

2

Personalized Therapy Sessions

The system creates customized exercise plans based on patient capabilities and recovery objectives.

3

Gamified Engagement

Patients engage with therapeutic games using the sensorized therapy ball, earning streaks and badges.

4

Progress Monitoring

Clinicians track adherence, performance metrics, and recovery progress through the cloud dashboard.
